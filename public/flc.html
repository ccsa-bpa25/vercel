Fall Leadership Conference



What is the FLC?

The Fall Leadership Conference (FLC) is an event where the leaders of each chapter come together for a day to learn and develop crucial leadership skills. There are breakout sessions where each group will complete an acitivity about the four pillars of leadership: trust, hope compassion, and stability.

The Four Pillars of Leadership

Trust

Trust is essential for leaders because it builds strong and reliable relationships. When leaders are trustworthy, they inspire loyalty and cooperation within a team.

Hope

Hope in leadership is about inspiring others to believe in a better future and to go beyond their limits. A leader motivates their team to work even in difficult times.

Stability

Stability means to be reliable and calm, even when things are tough. A leader helps their team feel secure and safe at all times, knowing they can count on them to make decisions.

Compassion

Compassion means caring about others' feelings and well-being. A compassionate leader listens, understands, and supports their team in a positive and caring environment.
