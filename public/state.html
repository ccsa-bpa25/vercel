State Leadership Conference

About the SLC


- In the State Leadership Conference, the BPA chapters of Delaware will compete for the chance to go to the NLC.


- In addition to competing, students can attend workshops, meet guest speakers, and network with peers.


- The SLC is a great oppurtunity to learn, grow, and connect with students with the same interests and goals.




SLC Info

Address: (Bally's Dover Casino) 1131 N Dupont Hwy, Dover, DE 19901
Date: February 5, 2025 (Middle Level) & February 6, 2025 (Secondary Level)
Time: 8:00 AM - 8:00 PM
Cost: $85 per student
Dress Code: Business Professional Attire
