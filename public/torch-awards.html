Torch Awards & Recognition

The BPA Torch Awards Program recognizes members who have demonstrated excellence in different BPA categories. This prestigious honor celebrates leadership, personal growth, and community involvement.



What are Torch Awards?

Students can complete activities in the seven torch categories and each activity is worth 5, 10, 15, or 20 points. Once a student has completed a certain amount of activities, they can log it in the online resume, in which then the advisor can submit it. Below are activities that can be completed in the seven categories:

Recognition


After a student reaches a specific number of points, they can get recognition. Executive requires 10 points in each category, Diplomat requires 30 points in each category, Statesman requires 50 points in each category, and Ambassador requires 70 points in each category.
