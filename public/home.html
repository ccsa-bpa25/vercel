What is BPA?


Business Professionals of America (BPA) is a national organization that allows students to build leadership, teamwork, and networking skills. BPA prepares students for careers in business, finance, marketing, and more.

BPA offers a variety of events such as website design, financial literacy, human resources, and many more! Students can participate in competitions leading up to national levels.









How to join BPA?


To join the CCSA ML BPA chapter, you must be in 7th or 8th grade who attends Cab Calloway School of the Arts. You can ask our advisor, Ms. Mercer to add you into our Schoology group for important events, meetings, and updates.
